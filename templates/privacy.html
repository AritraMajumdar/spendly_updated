Privacy Policy

How Spendly collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide the service: authenticating your account, storing your expenses, and showing you summaries and reports of your own spending.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in our database and is not shared with third parties. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Cookies and Sessions

Spendly uses session cookies to keep you signed in. These cookies are necessary for the app to function and are not used for advertising or tracking across other sites.

5. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or other third parties. Any future use of third-party services will be reflected in an updated version of this policy.

6. Your Rights

You can view, update, or delete your expense data at any time from within the app. If you would like your account and associated data fully removed, contact the project maintainers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to the project maintainers.
